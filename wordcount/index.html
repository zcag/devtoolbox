About this word counter

This free online word counter instantly analyzes your text as you type. It counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and lines.

Reading time is estimated at 200 words per minute (average adult reading speed). Speaking time uses 130 words per minute, useful for preparing speeches or presentations.

The Flesch-Kincaid Reading Ease score ranges from 0 to 100. Higher scores mean the text is easier to read: 90-100 is very easy (5th grade), 60-70 is standard (8th-9th grade), and below 30 is very difficult (college graduate level).

Keyword density shows the most frequently used words, excluding common stop words. This is useful for SEO analysis and content optimization.

Everything runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No text is sent to any server.

DevToolBox — all processing happens in your browser. No data is collected or transmitted.
